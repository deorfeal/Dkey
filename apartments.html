Luxurious Beachfront Living in Dubai Islands

Discover the final opportunity to own a luxury waterfront apartment at Bay Grove Residences in Dubai, located in the heart of the prestigious Dubai Islands. This last phase offers 360-degree water views, direct beach access to Crystal Beach, and resort-style amenities designed for premium coastal living.

Set at the tip of the bay, these residences offer uninterrupted 360-degree water views, direct access to Crystal Beach, and seamless entry to the vibrant Beach lifestyle – where beachfront dining, water sports, and curated events define everyday living.

Choose from a limited collection of 1, 2, 3, and 4-bedroom apartments, plus exclusive 4-bedroom residences and a luxury penthouse in Building 15 — the crown jewel of the development.
